Thank you for choosing us. We want to ensure you for your satisfaction with our products. Please review our return and refund policy carefully.

1. Return Eligibility

Customers may return eligible items within 24 hours from the date of receiving their orders.

2. Eligible Items

To be eligible for a return, items must be in their original condition, unused, and in the original packaging. Items that show signs of use or damage may not be eligible for a refund.

3. Initiation of Return

To initiate a return, customers must contact us within 24 hours of receiving the order. Please provide your order number and a detailed reason for the return.

4. Return Shipping

Customers are responsible for the cost of return shipping. Please ensure that the items are securely packaged to prevent damage during transit. We recommend using a trackable shipping method.

5. Inspection and Processing

Once we receive the returned items, our team will inspect them for eligibility. If the items meet our return criteria, we will process the refund. Refunds will be issued to the original payment method used for the purchase.

6. Refund Timeframe

Refunds will be processed within 7 business days of receiving the returned items.

7. Exchanges

We do not offer exchanges. If you need a different item, please initiate a return and place a new order.

8. Damaged or Defective Items

Please record every parcel before opening the packaging. If you receive a damaged or defective item or missing items within 24 hours, please contact us immediately. We will arrange for a replacement or refund as appropriate.
